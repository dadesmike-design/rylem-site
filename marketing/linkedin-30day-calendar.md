### Post 4 — Monday

Your team absorbs the extra work.

And you're back at square one with a new vendor.

Happy contractors = reliable delivery for you. It's that simple.

### Post 6 — Friday
**Theme:** What hiring managers get wrong about staffing

The biggest mistake I see hiring managers make:

They evaluate staffing firms on fill time and bill rate.

Those matter. But they're table stakes.

The real question is: what happens after the placement?

Does your staffing partner disappear? Or do they stay engaged — with the contractor, with you — to make sure it works?

The cost of a failed placement is 3–5x the cost of the original hire.

Find a partner who's as invested in the outcome as you are.
