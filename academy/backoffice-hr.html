🚪 IV. Offboarding
👤 Owners: Aya, Lanni, Mary
📄Offboarding Process▼
  • Follow the offboarding training document step by step
  • Coordinate between HR (Aya), Payroll (Lanni), and Equipment (Mary)
  • Ensure nothing falls through the cracks — use the checklist
✉️Separation Letters (US)▼
  • Send separation letter using the standard template
  • Include: last day of work, final pay info, benefits continuation (COBRA), equipment return instructions
  • Send on or before the last day of assignment
💵Final Pay Processing▼
  • US final pay: Lanni processes — ensure all hours are captured
  • PH final pay: Rose processes
  • Check state laws for final pay deadlines — some states require same-day or next-day
  • Verify no outstanding advances or deductions before processing
⚠️
State Law

Final pay timing varies by state. California = immediate. Washington = next regular payday. Know the rules for each contractor's state.

💻Equipment Return (Mary)▼
  • Track all company-issued equipment in the master tracker
  • Send return instructions with prepaid shipping label if remote
  • Follow up until equipment is confirmed received
  • Flag unreturned equipment to HR lead after 14 days
