📈 II. Month-End Reporting
👤 Owner: Rose

All month-end reports are due by the 5th business day of the following month. No exceptions.

⏱️Timecard Report (Parts 1 & 2)▼
  • Part 1: Pull all timecards for the month, verify hours match approved totals
  • Part 2: Reconcile against payroll to ensure everything ties out
  • Flag any discrepancies before moving to revenue reporting
🎥

  • Compile total revenue for the month by client and contract type
  • Break down: Contract VMS, Contract Non-VMS, Contract GDM, Placement Fees
  • Compare against budget and prior month
