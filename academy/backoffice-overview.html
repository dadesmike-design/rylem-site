🚨 Non-Negotiable Rules
  • Accuracy over speed. Payroll errors affect real people's paychecks. Double-check everything.
  • Deadlines are deadlines. Biweekly payroll, 15th/30th PH payroll, month-end reports — no excuses.
  • Document everything. If it's not in the tracker, it didn't happen.
  • Confidentiality is absolute. HR data, compensation, benefits — never share outside the team.
  • Follow the process. Every task has a documented workflow. Use it. Don't improvise on payroll.
  • Escalate early. If something looks wrong — a payment, a timecard, a tax document — flag it immediately.
